Welcome to your Accessibility Journey

"The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect."

At Accessible by Design, we believe accessibility isn’t a checklist—it’s a mindset. This site is your launchpad to understanding, applying, and advocating for accessibility in learning and design. Whether you are an instructional designer, educator, developer, or simply curious, we’ll guide you from foundational concepts to practical implementation—step by step.

Explore interactive tools, real-world case studies, simulations, and expert insights to build learning experiences that reach everyone—regardless of ability, background, or context.

Let's make accessibility second nature—not an afterthought.
